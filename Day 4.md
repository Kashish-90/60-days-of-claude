Key Learning

One of the biggest insights from today's challenge was understanding how breaking down complex goals into smaller, structured steps makes career planning more practical and achievable.

Chain-of-Thought prompting helps AI reason through problems systematically, leading to more detailed and actionable outputs. This approach can be applied not only to career planning but also to learning, problem-solving, and decision-making.

---

My Biggest Insight

AI is most effective when provided with clear context and structured prompts. By using a step-by-step prompting approach, I was able to receive a more personalized and meaningful career roadmap that highlighted areas for growth and future opportunities.
