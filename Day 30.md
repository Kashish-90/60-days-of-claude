---

💡 Key Takeaway

A supply chain is much more than moving products from one place to another.

Every decision creates trade-offs:

Lower costs can increase risks.

Faster delivery can raise expenses.

More inventory improves availability but increases storage costs.

Sustainable choices may require additional investment.


The challenge is finding the right balance between efficiency, customer satisfaction, and profitability.
