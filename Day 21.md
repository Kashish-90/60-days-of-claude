# 🚀 Day 21 of 60 Days AB Talks Claude Challenge

## 📌 Project: Digital Privacy Dashboard

Today, I worked on a **Digital Privacy Dashboard** using Claude AI. This project focused on understanding how personal data is exposed online and how privacy metrics can be visualized through an interactive dashboard.

### 🔍 Key Learning Outcomes

- Understanding Digital Footprint Scores
- Analyzing Privacy Scores and Risk Levels
- Exploring Exposure Heatmaps
- Reviewing Company Exposure Rankings
- Creating Interactive HTML Dashboards
- Learning how AI can assist in data visualization and privacy awareness

### 🛠️ Technologies Used

- HTML5
- CSS3
- JavaScript
- Claude AI

### 📖 Project Overview

The Digital Privacy Dashboard helps users visualize their online presence and privacy risks through different metrics and graphical representations. It provides insights into digital exposure, privacy scoring, and company-level data exposure rankings.

### 🎯 What I Learned

This challenge enhanced my understanding of:

- Data Privacy Concepts
- Dashboard Design Principles
- AI-Assisted Development
- Data Visualization Techniques
- Real-World Applications of Web Development

### 🌱 Reflection

Every day of this challenge introduces a new concept and practical implementation. Today's project highlighted the importance of digital privacy and demonstrated how technology can help users better understand and manage their online presence.
