🚀 Day 16 – Stock Fundamental Research Custom Skill in Claude

Part of the AB Talks – 60 Days Claude Challenge

📌 Overview

On Day 16, I built a Custom Skill in Claude designed for Stock Fundamental Research. This skill helps automate company analysis by generating detailed reports, comparing stocks, evaluating valuations, identifying risks, and reviewing ownership patterns.

The goal is to create a reusable AI workflow that transforms complex financial data into structured, actionable insights for investors and researchers.

🎯 What This Skill Can Do

Generate comprehensive stock research reports

Compare multiple companies side-by-side

Analyze financial statements and key metrics

Review valuation indicators

Examine shareholding and ownership trends

Identify business and market risks

Summarize investment strengths and concerns


🛠️ Implementation Steps

1. Read the provided resources.


2. Open Claude.


3. Set effort level to Low or Medium.


4. Navigate to the Skills section.


5. Create a new Custom Skill.


6. Name the skill: stock-fundamental-research


7. Add the provided description.


8. Paste the provided instructions.


9. Save the Custom Skill.


10. Test using stocks such as:



TCS

Infosys

Reliance Industries

HDFC Bank

Tata Motors


11. Generate reports or compare stocks.



💡 Key Learnings

Building specialized AI workflows for finance

Designing effective prompts and instructions

Automating stock research tasks

Improving consistency in investment analysis

Leveraging AI for data-driven decision-making


📈 Why It Matters

Custom AI skills can significantly reduce the time spent gathering and organizing financial information. By creating domain-specific workflows, analysts and investors can focus more on insights and less on repetitive research tasks.
