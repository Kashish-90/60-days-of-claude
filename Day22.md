🚀 Day 22 of 60 Days AB Talks Claude Challenge

AI-Powered Startup Validation Report

Today, I explored how AI can be used to validate startup ideas like a professional VC analyst. I created a comprehensive Startup Validation Report covering market opportunity, founder-market fit, customer personas, competitor analysis, TAM/SAM/SOM calculations, risk assessment, and a strategic 30-day action plan.

📌 Key Learnings

Validate the problem before building the solution.

Understand your Ideal Customer Profile (ICP).

Analyze competitors to discover market gaps.

Estimate market size using TAM, SAM, and SOM frameworks.

Evaluate Founder-Market Fit objectively.

Identify risks early and create mitigation strategies.

Build a data-driven roadmap for execution.
