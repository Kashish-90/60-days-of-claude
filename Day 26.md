🏥 Prior Authorization Workflow Simulator
Today I explored how AI can be used to simulate and automate a real healthcare workflow.
What I Built
A complete Prior Authorization Workflow Simulator that guides users through the authorization process from patient intake to final approval or appeal.
Features
Patient scenario creation
Workflow stage progression
Document collection and validation
Authorization request assembly
Payer submission process
Approval, denial, and appeal outcomes
Interactive HTML application
What I Learned
Workflow design principles
Process mapping and automation
Prompt engineering for complex applications
Structuring large AI-generated projects
User experience considerations in workflow systems
Biggest Insight
AI becomes significantly more powerful when it is used to model real-world business processes rather than only generating isolated pieces of code.
