# Day 13/60 – AB Talks Claude Challenge 🚀

## Overview
Day 13 of the **AB Talks 60 Days Claude Challenge** focused on leveraging **Claude Connectors with Indeed** to create an AI-powered job search workflow. The goal was to use Claude as a career assistant to identify relevant opportunities, analyze matches, and uncover skill gaps.

## What I Accomplished
- Connected **Indeed** with **Claude Connectors**
- Created a professional profile using structured prompts
- Defined targeted job search criteria
- Enabled Claude to discover matching job opportunities
- Reviewed opportunity match scores
- Explored skill-gap recommendations for career growth

## Key Takeaway
AI can significantly improve the job search process by automating opportunity discovery, providing personalized recommendations, and helping identify areas for professional development.

## Learning Reflection
Today's challenge demonstrated how AI can move beyond simple conversations and become a practical tool for career planning, job matching, and continuous learning.
