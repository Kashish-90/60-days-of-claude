# Day 17/60 – AB Talks Claude Challenge 🚀

Today’s challenge was all about turning raw vehicle data into a powerful analytics dashboard using Claude. From CSV processing and KPI calculations to fuel-cost comparisons, maintenance insights, CO₂ analysis, E85 economics, and interactive SVG visualizations—this was a great exercise in data storytelling and dashboard design.

## 📌 Key Learnings

- ✅ Data-driven decision making
- ✅ Cost-per-km and maintenance analytics
- ✅ Fuel efficiency comparisons
- ✅ Interactive dashboard development
- ✅ Converting complex data into actionable insights

## 🎯 Challenge Highlights

- Processed vehicle datasets from CSV files
- Generated analytical KPIs for fuel and maintenance costs
- Compared multiple fuel types and their economic impact
- Evaluated environmental metrics through CO₂ analysis
- Explored E85 fuel economics and break-even calculations
- Designed responsive dashboard concepts with SVG visualizations

Every day of this challenge is helping me strengthen my AI prompting, analytics, visualization, and problem-solving skills. Building practical projects while learning AI tools is proving to be an effective way to grow both technically and professionally.
