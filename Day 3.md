Day 3 – 60 Days AB Talks Claude Challenge

Role-Based Prompting

Objective

The objective of this task was to understand and explore Role-Based Prompting, a prompt engineering technique where a specific role or persona is assigned to an AI model to influence the style, perspective, and quality of its responses.

Activity Performed

I asked Claude the same question in three different scenarios:

1. Without assigning any role.
2. Assigning the role of a Founder.
3. Assigning the role of a Developer.

I then compared the responses to observe how the assigned persona affected the AI's output.

Key Learnings

- Role-Based Prompting helps generate more focused and context-aware responses.
- Different personas produce different perspectives for the same problem.
- A Founder persona emphasizes business strategy, growth, and market opportunities.
- A Developer persona focuses on technical implementation, architecture, and tools.
- Proper prompt design can significantly improve the quality and relevance of AI-generated responses.

Conclusion

This exercise demonstrated the power of prompt engineering and highlighted how assigning a role can guide AI models to provide responses tailored to specific goals and domains. Understanding Role-Based Prompting is an important skill for effectively leveraging AI tools in real-world applications.
